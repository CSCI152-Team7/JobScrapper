  • CSCI-152 Project - Spring 2020
  • The idea behind JobScrapper(name pending) web app is to streamline the job hunting experience. Currently, there are many sites dedicated to finding jobs. However, these sites only post information they receive from potential employers. There is no standardized format for employers to follow, and that oftentimes leads to job posting with unclear and unnecessary information. Through the use of machine learning, text summarization, and web scraping, our web app will make the job-hunting process less tedious for potential job-hunters.
